Why should I consider coworking?

Happier working day

Studies have shown that employees with more control over their working hours, location and tools are far more engaged, productive and happy.

More connections

In a traditional office setting with fixed desks, you generally see the same people every day. In a coworking environment, you can meet people and connections from other firms, locations and industries.

Save money

Really? Yes. A typical desk in an office environment is only occupied 40-60% of the time. In a coworking space you only pay for those days you are there working. If you take vacation, travel for work or work from home you are not paying for an empty desk.
