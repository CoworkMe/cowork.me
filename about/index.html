A little bit about us

We want to improve the working lifestyle for all knowledge workers.

We have spent the prime of our young working years working for great companies, but in almost all cases this work took place in standard corporate offices - i.e. uninspiring and boring. We managed to work and achieve despite this environment not because of it.

But hey, we don't hold a grudge. Creating an inspiring workplace for hundreds or thousands of knowledge workers and creative people which also fits each person's individual working style and personality type is unfathomably hard to achieve - keeping everyone happy is simply not possible, despite management's best efforts.

Cowork Me is our initiative to give you control over your workspace, while still remaining part of your existing company. We let you make the decisions about where and how you work, so you can focus on the work you do as part of the company you work for. Don't underestimate the empowerment that comes from choosing your work place to fit your personality and the work you do.
